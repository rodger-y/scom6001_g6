Cold-smoked salmon

Cold-smoked salmon has a strong premium and health halo because of its omega-3 content and high-quality protein. 1

It is glossy, clean, and usually sold as ready to eat at high prices. 2

Production and storage do not efficiently kill Listeria. 2 3

The smoking process potentially introduces carcinogenic chemicals, including PAHs, which are a known concern in smoked fish. Long-term consumption in large volumes could therefore have health impacts. 4 5

Salmon can also carry environmental contaminants, although it is generally considered lower in mercury than large predatory fish. 1

Tasmania and Norway are common sources of salmon products. 6 7
