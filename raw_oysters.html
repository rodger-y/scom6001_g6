Raw oysters

Oysters are a nutritious shellfish. Raw oyster consumption is common in many cultures. 1

Oysters can contain microbial risks, such as Vibrio, that can lead to severe or fatal consequences. 1 2 3

Microbial risks are not distinguishable by appearance or smell. 1

Raw oysters are usually paired with wine at consumption. 4

Oysters are rich in purines, which can contribute to gout flares. 5

Oyster consumption has a long-running tradition; it has been depicted in classical literature, and is associated with freshness and luxurious lifestyle on social media. 4
