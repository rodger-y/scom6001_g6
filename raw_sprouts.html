Raw sprouts

Raw sprouts are fresh and plant-based. They are also a source of fibre. They are affordable in both daily cooking and catering services. 1 2

They bear risk because seeds may already be contaminated. The warm and humid conditions used for sprouting can amplify pathogens. 1 2

It is difficult for consumers to identify these pathogens, as sprouts can look crisp and clean while still being associated with repeated outbreaks. 1 2 3
