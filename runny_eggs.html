Runny eggs

Eggs are a common source of protein. They appear frequently in dietary guides and fitness influencer videos. They are accessible in supermarkets, and prices vary. 1 2

Eggs also have a common appearance in ethical debates about animal rights. 3

They are a source of choline and other nutrients. 2

A soft yolk or runny texture is culturally normal and sometimes preferred for aesthetics, but raw or lightly cooked eggs can contain Salmonella unless they are pasteurised. Pasteurisation is not guaranteed in all eggs. 1 4

The relationship between high egg intake and cardiovascular health remains debated. 2
