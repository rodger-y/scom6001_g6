Kimchi

Kimchi is a Korean fermented food with major cultural significance. 1 2

When removed from packaging, sour smell, bubbles, pressure release, and cloudiness are normal. 2 3

It is widely discussed as a functional food with possible gut, metabolic, and anti-inflammatory benefits, though the strength of evidence varies. 1 4

When fermentation or handling does not meet standard, it risks contamination. 5 6
