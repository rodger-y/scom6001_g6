14. Home-brew alcohol

Home-brew alcohol, or moonshine, is alcohol brewed on a small scale at home. ₁
It often carries a homemade, traditional, or artisanal sentiment. ₁
Improper handling may result in methanol and lead (Pb) contamination, which is not distinguishable by appearance or smell. ₁ ₂ ₃
Methanol and Pb contaminants can result in blindness, neurological injury, and death. ₁ ₂ ₃
Ethanol itself, consumed in large quantities, poses strong health risks and may result in societal harm depending on the context. ₄
