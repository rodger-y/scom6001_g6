8. Cold-smoked salmon

Cold-smoked salmon has a strong premium and health halo because of its omega-3 content and high-quality protein. ₁
It is glossy, clean, and usually sold as ready to eat at high prices. ₂
Production and storage do not efficiently kill Listeria. ₂ ₃
The smoking process potentially introduces carcinogenic chemicals, including PAHs, which are a known concern in smoked fish. Long-term consumption in large volumes could therefore have health impacts. ₄ ₅
Salmon can also carry environmental contaminants, although it is generally considered lower in mercury than large predatory fish. ₁
Tasmania and Norway are common sources of salmon products. ₆ ₇
