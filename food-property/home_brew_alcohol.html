Home-brew alcohol

Home-brew alcohol, or moonshine, is alcohol brewed on a small scale at home. 1

It often carries a homemade, traditional, or artisanal sentiment. 1

Improper handling may result in methanol and lead (Pb) contamination, which is not distinguishable by appearance or smell. 1 2 3

Methanol and Pb contaminants can result in blindness, neurological injury, and death. 1 2 3

Ethanol itself, consumed in large quantities, poses strong health risks and may result in societal harm depending on the context. 4
