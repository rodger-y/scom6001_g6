12. Tuna

Tuna products are common in the market, including sashimi and canned products. ₁
Canned tuna is often associated with health culture due to its high protein content. ₁
Tuna sashimi is often sold as a premium food at high prices. ₁
Some species of tuna have been reported to contain relatively high concentrations of mercury (Hg), which can contribute to mercury poisoning after heavy long-term fish consumption. Australia reports on mercury under the Minamata Convention, but that does not mean every tuna product is individually tested and reported to the United Nations. ₂ ₃ ₄
Tuna is also linked to scombroid poisoning (histamine fish poisoning) when improperly refrigerated. ₅ ₆
