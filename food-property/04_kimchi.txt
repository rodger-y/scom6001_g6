4. Kimchi

Kimchi is a Korean fermented food with major cultural significance. ₁ ₂
When removed from packaging, sour smell, bubbles, pressure release, and cloudiness are normal. ₂ ₃
It is widely discussed as a functional food with possible gut, metabolic, and anti-inflammatory benefits, though the strength of evidence varies. ₁ ₄
When fermentation or handling does not meet standard, it risks contamination. ₅ ₆
