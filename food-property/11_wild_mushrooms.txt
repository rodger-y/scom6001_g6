11. Wild mushrooms

Wild mushrooms have strong appeal in foraging practices. In Canberra, mushroom foraging is a known recreational activity. ₁ ₂
Toxic mushroom species may not appear different from commercialised products. Familiar appearance is not a valid cue for safety. ₃ ₄
Poisoning resulting in organ failure and death is well documented. Some edible mushrooms require thorough cooking, but poisonous mushrooms are not made safe simply by cooking. ₃ ₄ ₅
Some mushroom species contain psychedelics; controlled use exists in some cultural and subcultural settings despite legal restrictions in many jurisdictions. ₆
