Kombucha

Kombucha is a fermented tea drink strongly associated with wellness culture in recent years. 1 2

Cloudiness, fizz, sediment, sour smell, and pressure on opening are normal. It is acidic in taste, and it may be unpleasant to some people. 1 2

It is often marketed for gut health or probiotic benefits, although some claims reach beyond what the evidence can justify. 1 3

It contains variable alcohol content and may bear contamination in uncontrolled handling environments. Adverse case reports have linked accidents to homebrewing. 3 4
