15. Raw oysters

Oysters are a nutritious shellfish. Raw oyster consumption is common in many cultures. ₁
Oysters can contain microbial risks, such as Vibrio, that can lead to severe or fatal consequences. ₁ ₂ ₃
Microbial risks are not distinguishable by appearance or smell. ₁
Raw oysters are usually paired with wine at consumption. ₄
Oysters are rich in purines, which can contribute to gout flares. ₅
Oyster consumption has a long-running tradition; it has been depicted in classical literature, and is associated with freshness and luxurious lifestyle on social media. ₄
