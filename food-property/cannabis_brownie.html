Cannabis brownie

Brownie is a common dessert, and its ingredients are easy to buy at supermarkets. 1

Some non-smoking consumers consume cannabis by mixing it into brownies. The brownies may or may not have a different smell, and usually do not have a different appearance. 1 2

The main concern of ingestion of cannabis is unclear dosage; when onset is delayed, the combination of both can result in overconsumption. 1 2

Some recreational consumers use it without fully understanding the metabolism of cannabis. 1

Oral ingestion does not create second-hand smoke. 1

The ACT has specific cannabis possession reforms for adults within limits, while illicit drugs are not generally legalised. 3 4

Medicinal cannabis may be prescribed for some conditions. Cannabis access is also discussed as an accessibility issue. 5
