1. Natto

Natto is a traditional Japanese food made with fermented soybeans. ₁
It is sticky, stringy, and has a strong odour, so cues that might look spoiled in other foods are normal here. However, its safety depends on controlled fermentation. ₁ ₂
It has possible cardiometabolic benefits; however, its high vitamin K may interact with warfarin anticoagulation (a blood thinner) and reduce its efficacy. It also contains soy allergen. ₁ ₃
